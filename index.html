About Us

Kallos Agency

We are model agency was founded in 2000 in Nigeria. Currently, Casting operates offices in Lagos and Anambra, alongside a trusted network of independent agencies around the world. Our models and talent have appeared in numerous marketing campaigns including Target, Levi's, Polo Jeans, Zara, Turkish Airlines and Tommy Hilfiger, to name a few. Kallos AGENCY was established to provide our clients with talent who have a strong social media presence. We look forward to working with you!
